Reading Book Buddy

This application was built with the idea of inspiring and encouraging youth to read more. It is an incentive program that allows students and childrent to enter a book they have read, give a rating for how challenging the book was, as well write a small report. Based on all information given the book is given a certain amount of points. To which student can then choose to collect prizes with.
